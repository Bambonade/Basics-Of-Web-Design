Yoga Classes

Gentle Hatha Yoga
Intedned for beginners and anyone wishing a grounded foundation in the practice of yoga, this 60 minute class of poses n asana (proper alignment and posture), pranayama (breath work), and guided meditation to foster your mind and body connection.
Yinyasa Yoga
Although designed for intermediate to advanced students, beginners are welcome to sample this 60 minute class that focuses on breath-synchronized movement--you will inhale and exhale as you flow energetically through yoga poses.
Restorative Yoga
This 90 minute class features very slow movements and long poses that are supported by a chair or wall. This calming, restorative experience is suitable for students of any level of experience. This Practice can be a perfect way to help rehabilitate an injury
